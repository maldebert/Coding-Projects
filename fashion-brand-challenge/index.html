Dresses for the ages

Lirika Matoshi dresses are the perfect head turner in any setting and occasion. Hapiness has more than a name, it has a shape. Those little bundles of joy are covered in glitter and tulle, have puffy sleeves, a beautiful silhouette and a fabulous twirl.

Yellow, blue or pink, the dresses come in many colors and many prints. Lemon, cherries, strawberries, blueberries, watermelon and bananas for the perfect festive look. Ideal during the warm season, it brings summer to the coldest winter.

A bit more about Lirika Matoshi

Lirika Matoshi launched in 2016 in New York City. As an emerging fashion brand, the designer offers a wide variety of garments and accessories, all creafted with luxury fabrics and maximum attention to details. The brand is made of 100% women and operates out of New York City (offices and showroom) and Kosovo (factory).

The Lirika Matoshi womenswear label is both effortlessly romantic and intrinsically sophisticated, adding a hint of sparkle to any everyday outfit. Recently Lirika Matoshi has also been added to 30 under 30 2022.
Fun fact: Lirika Matoshi is the sister of Kosovo-based fashion and gown designer Teuta Matoshi.
